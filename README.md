# Phys-Koi
Based on the Physics Explained Through a Video Game/Newton's First and Third Laws Wikibook; a player is able to maneuver a koi fish througout. As such, the fish is able to spin and move gently across the body of water and also interact respectively with the things surrounding him.

## Story
You play as a happy little koi fish, who just enjoys to swim and live life, explore the pond you reside in while experimenting and demostrating with three basic Physics concepts:
* Dynamics (Newton’s second law)
* Collisions (conservation of momentum)
* Orbital Mechanics (Newtonian gravitation)
Swim around and try out different things, this way you can understand in a simple but comprehensive way these concepts. Enjoy Phys - Koi.

## Controls/ Game Mechanis
When starting the game, the player presses the Play button to enter the main scene. The player controls a fish using the WASD or Arrow keys to move around freely in the pond environment. Interactive objects float in the water and can be pushed by the fish, demonstrating Newton’s laws of motion through collisions and reactions. A whirlpool is present in the scene, pulling objects strongly in a spiral toward its center while also affecting the fish with a lighter suction that slows movement but still allows escape. The mechanics highlight inertia, action-reaction, and external forces, giving the player a simple but clear experience of physics in action. Test out the different properties of the things around the pond. Explore and experiment.
